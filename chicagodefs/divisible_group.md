An [abelian](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/abelian) [group](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/group) $(A,+)$ is **divisible** if for every $d\in \mathbb N$ and for every $a\in A$, there exists $a'\in A$ such that $da' = a$.
