Let $F$ be a [field](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/field) of [characteristic](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/characteristic_of_a_field) $p$. Then the map $x\mapsto x^p$ is called the **Frobenius endomorphism**.
