Let $(X,*)$ be a [magma](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/magma) with [identity element](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/identity_element) $e$. An element $a\in X$ is a **right inverse element** for $b\in X$ if $a * b=e$.
