Let $u,v$ be elements of the [group](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/group) $G$. Define $[u,v] = uvu^{-1}v^{-1}$. The [subgroup](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/subgroup) of $G$ [generated](https://defsmath.github.io/DefsMath/generate_a_Ïƒ-algebra) by $[u,v]$ for all pairs $u,v\in G$ is denoted $[G,G]$ and is called the **commutator subgroup** of $G$.
