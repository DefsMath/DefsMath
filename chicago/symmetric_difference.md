The **symmetric difference** of two sets $A$ and $B$ is given by $$A\triangle B = (A\setminus B)\cup(B\setminus A) = (A\cup B)\setminus (A\cap B).$$ It is the set of elements that are in exactly one of $A$ or $B$.
